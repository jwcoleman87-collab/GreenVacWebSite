Why Compact

Built Small, On Purpose

The whole rig is built around one idea: get into the spots a full-size truck can't, then do careful, precise work once we're in there. That's the job we're set up for — and it's most of what we do.

Fits Where Trucks Can't

Standard gates, narrow side passages, between buildings, around established gardens — the trailer-mounted rig works in space a rigid hydrovac truck simply can't reach.

Less Disruption on Site

A smaller footprint means minimal spoil and no tearing up access just to get the machine in. We work around what's already there, carefully.

Usually No Street Closure

Working from the kerb or the property, most residential jobs don't need a traffic management plan — saving time and cost before we've even started digging.

The further out, the less competition

Most hydrovac operators don't want the regional job. Too far, too much hassle, not worth the quote. That's fine by us — we've been doing those jobs for years and we'll keep doing them. Remote doesn't scare us. It just means we're probably the only ones who called back.

Regular trade clients travel well

Plumbers, sparkies, civil contractors — if you've got a job outside the ACT, just ring. We travel regularly for trade clients because the ongoing relationship matters more than the distance. We'll tell you straight if the site works.
