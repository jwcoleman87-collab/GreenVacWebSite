## Services

Hydrovac and Hydro Excavation
Compact hydro excavation for targeted digging around homes, commercial sites, services, paths, gardens, and tight access areas across Canberra ACT and Southern NSW.

Non-Destructive Digging
Pressurised water and vacuum excavation to expose underground services without the damage risk of mechanical digging.

Utility Potholing
Precise potholing to verify the exact location and depth of underground pipes, cables, conduits, and services before digging or installation work.

Precision Trenching
Short, targeted hydrovac trenching for electrical, plumbing, irrigation, data, and service runs where clean edges and minimal disruption matter.

Leak Detection Support
Careful hydrovac exposure around suspected water, irrigation, stormwater, and service leaks so plumbers and technicians can inspect and repair safely.

Pit and Cattle Grid Cleaning
Vacuum cleanout for service pits, valve pits, drainage pits, sludge, debris, and cattle grid chambers.

Tight Access Excavation
Compact hydrovac work for narrow side access, courtyards, retaining walls, fence lines, garden beds, stairs, and areas larger equipment cannot reach.
